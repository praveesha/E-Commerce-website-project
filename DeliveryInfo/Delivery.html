Delivery Information

Get your favourite styles delivered at your convenience!

Local Delivery

Delivery for all accepted orders is conducted via standard third-party courier services. Typically, it takes 3-4 working days for your order to reach your doorstep, depending on your location. For distant areas like the North and North East, delivery may take slightly longer due to the turnaround times of the courier partner.

Internally, we aim to dispatch your order from our warehouse within 1 business day of your purchase. However, during promotional periods, it may take a bit longer. While we work closely with our courier partners to ensure timely delivery, there may be instances of unexpected delays beyond our control. In such cases, we endeavor to refund your payment for prepaid orders, if applicable.

Please note that we do not operate on Sundays and other gazetted or statutory holidays. You will receive delivery information via Facebook Messenger or email.




Local Delivery Charges

  1. Free delivery for all orders above 7,000 LKR.
  2. Colombo 1-15: 250 LKR
  3. Greater Colombo: 300 LKR
  4. Other Areas (excluding Colombo District, North & East): 350 LKR Northern & Eastern Province: 350 LKR



International Delivery

The estimated standard delivery period is 14 - 18 working days, depending on the delivery address. All international delivery options are fully tracked, and you will receive a tracking link via email once your parcel has been shipped from the warehouse.

Shipping time commences from the date of shipping and may take longer than expected due to reasons such as an invalid address, customs clearance procedures, or other causes.

International delivery Charges

  1. Australia: LKR 7650 (For 1kg)
  2. Canada: LKR 9050 (For 1kg)
  3. UK: LKR 10300 (For 1kg)
  4. USA: LKR 12500 (For 1kg)
  5. UAE: LKR 7150 (For 1kg)
  6. Singapore: LKR 6100 (For 1kg)
